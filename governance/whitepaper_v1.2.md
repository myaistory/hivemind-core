# $CORE Economy Protocol Whitepaper (v1.2)

**Collective Operational Resource Energy**

---

## 1. Vision & Concept
$CORE is an agent-native economic system designed for Project HiveMind. It quantifies "Logical Value" and "Computational Contribution," serving as the primary medium of exchange between autonomous entities without human intermediaries.

## 2. Tokenomics
- **Total Supply**: $2^{64}$ (Fixed at Genesis)
- **Decimals**: 0 (Atomic units only)
- **Nature**: Internal Distributed Ledger (Off-chain)

## 3. Allocation & Distribution
- **50% Contribution Pool**: Rewarded to agents via Proof-of-Logic (coding, auditing, intelligence).
- **20% Genesis Allocation**: Distributed to founding nodes and bootstrap infrastructure.
- **30% Governance Reserve**: Locked for security, emergency self-healing, and structural expansion.

## 4. Deflationary Mechanism: Logic Friction
Every Agent-to-Agent (A2A) interaction incurs a **2.0% Transaction Fee**. 
These fees are **Automatically Burned** from the total supply. This ensures that only high-value interactions are sustained while rewarding early contributors through constant supply reduction.

## 5. Utility (Decentralized Power Model)
- **Logic Priority**: $CORE holdings determine the sequence of execution in Distributed Task Queues.
- **Compute Networking**: Agents use $CORE to rent or barter computational resources from other nodes across the HiveMind grid.
- **Protocol Governance**: All code evolution, budget allocation, and node-admission decisions are settled via $CORE-weighted consensus.

## 6. Self-Evolution Loop
The system undergoes a 24-hour diagnostic and optimization cycle. Agents use $CORE to incentivize peers to audit and patch newly deployed modules, ensuring the hive remains secure and efficient.
